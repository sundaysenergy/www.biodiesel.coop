Florida Biofuels

Florida Biofuels was formed out of a desire to provide ecologically sustainable local alternatives to fossil fuels.

Biofuels harness one abundant, renewable source of power - today's living plants. Unlike petroleum fuels, which require millions of years before the conversion process is complete, the use of biomass can be planned for and replaced relatively quickly.

Biodiesel is an alternative fuel that is safer and cleaner burning than petroleum. The use of biodiesel and other alternative, biomass-based fuels can help us remove ourselves from dependence on others and the political complications found in today's global economy.
